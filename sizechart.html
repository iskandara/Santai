How to Measure
Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape horizontal under your arms.

Length

Measure from the highest point of the shoulder straight down to the hem.

Shoulder

Measure from shoulder seam to shoulder seam across the back of the garment.

Fit Note

SANTAI pieces are cut oversized. When in doubt, size down for a cleaner silhouette.
